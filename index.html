Carter Sifferman

I am a Computer Science PhD student at University of Wisconsin - Madison, advised by Michael Gleicher and Mohit Gupta.

My work primarily focuses on utilizing low-level techniques from computational imaging to improve robot perception. I am currently most interested in time-of-flight proximity sensors for up-close and distrbuted robot sensing.
